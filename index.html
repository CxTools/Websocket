WSServer is highly extendable. You can add your own extensions and subprotocols as long as they adhere to a simple API.

WSServer runs out of the box with no dependencies. However to enable features such as WebSockets over TLS (wss://) and compression (permessage-deflate extension), OpenSSL and zlib must be installed.
